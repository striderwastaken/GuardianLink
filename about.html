About Us

About Us

Guardian Link was built to bring peace of mind to every parent — especially those with hearing challenges. Our smart baby monitoring system detects crying, plays soothing lullabies, and sends real-time alerts to ensure no cry goes unheard.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make parenting accessible, safe, and inclusive. By combining AI with empathy, we’re helping parents stay connected and responsive — even in moments when they can’t be right beside their child.

Our Story

Guardian Link started as a small idea — a way to help hearing-impaired parents detect their baby’s cries. With time, it evolved into a mission to make caregiving smarter, safer, and more compassionate.
