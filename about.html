About Us

About Us

• At Guardian Link, we believe that no parent should ever miss their baby’s cry. Our journey began with a simple question — how can parents who are hearing impaired or busy know when their baby needs them? That question inspired us to build a smart solution that combines compassion with technology. Guardian Link uses machine learning to detect a baby’s cry, plays a gentle lullaby to comfort them, and instantly alerts parents through their smartphone.
• We’re not just creating a product — we’re building peace of mind for families everywhere.

Our Mission

To make parenting more inclusive, safe, and stress-free through intelligent technology. We aim to ensure that no baby’s cry goes unheard and no parent feels disconnected from their child’s needs.

Our Story

• Guardian Link was born out of a real experience. During a family gathering, our founder noticed that his hearing-impaired cousin couldn’t hear his baby crying in another room. That moment sparked an idea — a system that could listen when parents couldn’t.
• From that spark came a mission — to build a device that listens, learns, and protects. What started as a small idea is now a growing innovation helping parents across the country.
